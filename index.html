R.O.S.S.
A.I. out of your imagination

R.O.S.S. is a Python-oriented programming language. It is designed to provide a user-friendly experience of interacting with an A.I. in the form of a virtual assistant.

This project is inspired from the likes of SIRI, ALEXA and GOOGLE ASSISTANT. It is programmed to minimize the work and reduce the time of users.
